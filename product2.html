Care Instructions

  • Place your plant in a location where it receives at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight each day.
  • Ajwain plants prefer warm temperatures between 70-85°F (21-29°C). Protect them from cold drafts and frost, as they are sensitive to low temperatures.
  • Ensure the soil pH is slightly acidic to neutral (around 6.5-7.0).
  • Reduce or eliminate fertilizer during the dormant season (fall and winter).li>
  • Prune your Ajwain plant regularly to encourage bushier growth and maintain its shape. Harvesting leaves for culinary use can also serve as a form of pruning.
  • For cuttings, take 3-4 inch (7-10 cm) stem cuttings, remove the lower leaves, and plant them in a well-draining mix. Keep them warm and moist until they root.
  • Treat any infestations promptly with insecticidal soap or neem oil.
  • With proper care and attention to its sunlight, water, and soil needs, your Ajwain plant can thrive and provide fresh, aromatic leaves for your culinary creations.
